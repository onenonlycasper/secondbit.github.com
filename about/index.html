"The entire point of the thing is to work with people I like, doing things I love"

Second Bit, LLC is not just a software company. It's a software team. We're not all that interested in selling software—our passion is for writing it.

At Second Bit, we believe in the same set of principles that drove the TMRC hackers at MIT. We think the best way to improve the world we live in is to give everyone the best tools we can, and to give them all the help we can. That's what drives our interest in Open Source.

We want to Do The Right Thing

In the beginning of its life, the hacker movement in MIT's AI labs had a concept of "right" that we like. It's the idea that some things are just right. A hack was "right" when it couldn't be any more succinct, couldn't be any more elegant. It was perfect.

At Second Bit, we like to take this idea of "right" and apply it to everything we do. Our applications are "right" when they're decentralised and elegantly structured. Our monetisation strategy (we are a company, we do need to make money to keep the lights on) is "right" when it's an enhancement, not something we ask users to swallow. Everything, from software to blog posts to the company itself, is subjected to this standard of "right".
